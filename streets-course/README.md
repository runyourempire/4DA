# STREETS: Developer Income Playbook

**7 modules. Build income streams from your developer skills.**

STREETS is a structured, hands-on playbook that transforms your existing technical skills into revenue-generating systems. No fluff, no motivation speeches — just code, tools, and real math.

## The Framework

| Letter | Module | Duration | Focus |
|--------|--------|----------|-------|
| **S** | Sovereign Setup | Weeks 1-2 | Configure your rig as a business asset |
| **T** | Technical Moats | Weeks 3-4 | Build what competitors can't easily copy |
| **R** | Revenue Engines | Weeks 5-8 | Eight ways to turn skills into income |
| **E** | Execution Playbook | Weeks 9-10 | Ship your first revenue engine |
| **E** | Evolving Edge | Weeks 11-12 | Stay ahead as markets shift |
| **T** | Tactical Automation | Week 13 | Automate repetitive income tasks |
| **S** | Stacking Streams | Week 14 | Combine engines for resilience |

## How to Read

**Sequential path:** Start with Module S and work through each module in order. Each builds on the previous.

**Reference path:** Jump to Module R (Revenue Engines) if you already have your infrastructure set up and want to start building immediately.

**In 4DA:** If you have the [4DA desktop app](https://4da.dev), open the Playbook tab for contextual intelligence — your signals get annotated with matching revenue engines.

## Templates

The `templates/` directory contains three worksheet templates you'll fill out during the playbook:

- **Sovereign Stack Document** — Your complete business infrastructure audit (Module S output)
- **Moat Map** — Visual map of your competitive advantages (Module T output)
- **Stream Stack** — Revenue stream portfolio plan (Module S2 output)
