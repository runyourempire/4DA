## Rules

1. No ask. No "please promote." No "would you feature."
2. Maximum 5-10 accounts total. Quality over volume.
3. No affiliate deals or payment offers.
4. Don't send until desktop app builds exist to share.
5. One message. No follow-up if no reply.
6. The npx command is the hook — they can try it in 30 seconds.
