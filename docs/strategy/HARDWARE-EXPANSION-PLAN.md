# 4DA Hardware Expansion Plan

> Status: EXPLORATION — no action until 4DA software ships and generates revenue.

## Phase 0: Ship 4DA (Now)

Nothing else matters until the software is live and generating revenue. Hardware plays require brand credibility, and brand credibility comes from a shipped product with paying users. Every hour spent on hardware strategy before launch is an hour stolen from the thing that funds everything else.

**Gate:** 4DA launched, Signal tier generating recurring revenue.

---

## Phase 1: Validate Hardware Demand (Post-Launch, Zero Cost)

Add a "Recommended Builds" page to 4da.ai. Three tiers:

| Tier | Target | GPU | Price Range |
|------|--------|-----|-------------|
| Starter | 7B-13B models | RTX 4060 Ti 16GB | ~$1,200 AUD |
| Pro | 30B-70B models | RTX 4090 24GB | ~$3,500 AUD |
| Station | Multi-model serving | 2x RTX 4090 or A6000 | ~$8,000+ AUD |

Affiliate links to PCPartPicker or AU retailers (Scorptec, PLE). Each build page has a "Notify me when 4DA Station launches" email capture.

**What this tells you:** Real demand signal at zero inventory risk. If 500 people sign up for notifications in 90 days, hardware is worth pursuing. If 50, it's not.

**Gate:** 500+ email signups in 90 days.

---

## Phase 2: Partner, Don't Build (When Demand Proves Out)

Contact one AU custom builder (Scorptec, PLE, Techfast — whoever does white-label). Negotiate:

- You spec the configs and brand them "4DA Station"
- They build, ship, and handle warranty
- You pre-install 4DA + Ollama + curated model pack
- You take 10-15% referral margin on hardware + ongoing software subscription

**Total capital outlay: near zero.** 4DA is a brand and software layer on top of existing supply chain.

**Gate:** First 10 units sold, positive margin confirmed.

---

## Phase 3: Financing via Existing Platforms

Integrate at checkout, not before:

- **AU:** Afterpay (up to $2K), Zip Pay/Zip Money (up to $5K+), Latitude (higher amounts)
- **US (if expanding):** Affirm, Klarna

These platforms handle credit assessment, compliance, collections. You pay 3-6% per transaction. No ACL, no ASIC reporting, no credit risk on your books.

**Action when ready:** Apply to Zip Money merchant program (handles the $3K-$8K range). Single integration, they own all the risk.

**Gate:** Checkout financing live, first financed purchase completed.

---

## Phase 4: Platform Play (12+ Months Post-Launch)

If Phase 2 works, expand into:

1. **Model marketplace** — curated, tested model configs for each hardware tier. Free models included, premium fine-tunes as upsell.
2. **Managed setup service** — remote configuration for SMBs wanting local inference servers. $500-$2K per engagement.
3. **4DA Fleet** — multi-machine management dashboard for teams running local inference across multiple rigs. This becomes the Team/Enterprise tier's killer feature.

**Gate:** Hardware revenue covering its own operational costs.

---

## Hard Rules

- Do NOT stockpile GPU inventory
- Do NOT apply for a credit licence
- Do NOT build a financing platform
- Do NOT announce hardware plans before software ships
- Do NOT spend money on any phase until the previous phase's gate is passed

---

## Strategic Logic

The core insight: local AI needs hardware, hardware needs software, nobody owns the full-stack local AI experience. 4DA's privacy-first, local-first identity is a natural foundation for hardware — this is vertical integration, not a pivot.

The sequence is: **ship software -> measure hardware interest for free -> partner if demand exists -> bolt on financing through existing platforms -> expand into platform if it compounds.**

Each phase is gated by evidence from the previous one. No phase requires more than marginal capital until proven.
