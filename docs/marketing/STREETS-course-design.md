*"From idea to deployed in 48 hours. No overthinking."*

- Legal minimum viable setup
  - LLC or sole proprietorship (country-dependent)

  - Terms of service template

### Module T — Tactical Automation
*"LLMs, agents, MCP, and cron jobs as force multipliers."*

**Week 12-13 | Scaling**

What you'll learn:
- The Automation Pyramid:
  ```
  Level 4: Autonomous agents (make decisions for you)
  Level 3: Intelligent pipelines (LLM-powered processing)
  Level 2: Scheduled automation (cron + scripts)
  Level 1: Manual with templates (copy-paste workflows)
  ```
- Level 1 → 2: Automating repetitive income tasks
  - Auto-publish content on schedule
  - Auto-respond to customer questions (with human review)
  - Auto-generate product variations
  - Auto-monitor competitors and adjust pricing
- Level 2 → 3: Adding LLM intelligence
  - Content pipelines: RSS → LLM summarize → newsletter draft → human review → publish
  - Customer research: scrape → LLM analyze → insight report → sell
  - Market monitoring: sources → LLM classify → alert on opportunity
- Level 3 → 4: Agent-based income generation
  - MCP servers that serve customers directly
  - AI agents that handle support tickets
  - Automated product creation based on demand signals
- The "Human-in-the-Loop" principle
  - Automation handles 80% → you handle the 20% that matters
  - Never fully automate customer-facing interactions (trust erosion)
  - Always review LLM output before publishing (reputation > speed)
- Cost optimization
  - When to use local LLM (free but slow) vs API (fast but $$$)
  - Batching strategies for API efficiency
  - The $200/mo API budget rule (if you can't ROI $200, fix the product)

**Key Deliverable:** One automated pipeline generating value without your active involvement.

**4DA Integration:** 4DA is itself a Level 3 automation — it ingests, scores, and surfaces without you lifting a finger. Build your income pipelines the same way. 4DA's attention report shows you where your time goes vs where it should go.

### Module S — Stacking Streams
*"One stream is a side hustle. Three streams is a business. Five streams is freedom."*

**Week 14-16 | Portfolio**

What you'll learn:
- The Income Portfolio concept:
  ```
  Stream 1: Quick cash (freelance/consulting) — pays bills NOW
  Stream 2: Growing asset (SaaS/product) — pays bills in 6 months
  Stream 3: Content compound (blog/newsletter/YouTube) — pays bills in 12 months
  Stream 4: Passive automation (bots/APIs/data) — pays while you sleep
  Stream 5: Equity play (open source → company) — long-term wealth
  ```
- How streams interact:
  - Consulting feeds product ideas (clients tell you what they'd pay for)
  - Content drives consulting leads (blog posts → inbound clients)
  - Products create content (launch stories, technical posts)
  - Automation supports everything (less manual work across all streams)
- The $10K/month milestone breakdown:
  - Consulting: 10 hrs/week × $200/hr = $8,000
  - Products: 200 customers × $15/mo = $3,000
  - Content: Newsletter + affiliates = $500-2,000
  - Mix and match to reach your number
- When to kill a stream:
  - < $100/mo after 6 months → kill or pivot
  - Negative ROI on time → automate or kill
  - Hating the work → kill (burnout kills all streams)
- Reinvestment strategy:
  - First $500/mo → better tooling (faster inference, more API credits)
  - First $2,000/mo → hire a VA for non-technical tasks
  - First $5,000/mo → consider going independent
  - First $10,000/mo → you have a real business

**Key Deliverable:** Your "Stream Stack" — a 12-month plan with 3-5 income streams, projected revenue, and monthly milestones.
