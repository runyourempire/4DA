> Send this individually to each person who emailed wanting to buy.
> Personal, direct, no marketing fluff.

## Template (Personalize the [name] and send from runyourempirehq@gmail.com)

```

shop.4da.ai
```

---

## Notes:
- Send from your personal email, not a marketing platform
- Don't CC/BCC multiple people — send individually
- Don't add a "use this discount code" — these people are already warm
- Don't ask them to share on social — let the product do the talking
- If they reply, respond personally. These are your first customers.
