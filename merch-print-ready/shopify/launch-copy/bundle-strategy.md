## Natural Bundle Psychology

The $120 free shipping threshold creates natural pairings:

### 2-Tee Bundle (most common path)
- Any 2 tees = $118 → just under threshold
- Customer adds cheapest shipping ($8) = $126 total
- OR they add a 3rd item to get free shipping → $177 total
- **Psychology:** "$2 short of free shipping" is a proven conversion nudge

### Tee + Layer Combos (highest margin path)
- Tee ($59) + Crewneck ($79) = $138 → FREE SHIPPING
- Tee ($59) + Hoodie ($89) = $148 → FREE SHIPPING
- **Psychology:** Customer already qualifies, feels good about the purchase

### Layer Bundle (premium path)
- Crewneck ($79) + Hoodie ($89) = $168 → FREE SHIPPING
- **Psychology:** "I'm already spending $79, might as well get the hoodie too"

## Future Pricing Considerations

### What NOT to do:
- Don't discount. You're not a sale brand. The prices are fair and the margins are healthy.
- Don't do BOGO. It cheapens the brand.
- Don't add a "compare at" price. You're not pretending this was ever $99.

### What TO consider (Phase 2+):
- **Sticker pack** ($12-15 AUD) — perfect cart filler for people at $110-115
- **Desk mat** ($45-55 AUD) — great second purchase, high margin
- **Pin** ($15-20 AUD) — another cart filler
- **Cap** ($39-49 AUD) — natural 2nd category expansion

### The Cart Filler Strategy
Right now, 2 tees = $118 = $2 short of free shipping. That's frustrating.
When you add a sticker pack at $12-15:
- 2 tees + sticker = $130-133 → free shipping, higher AOV, customer feels smart
- This is the single highest-impact product addition you can make

## When to Consider Paid (Not Now)

Don't run ads until:
- [ ] You have 10+ orders fulfilled successfully
- [ ] At least 3 reviews with photos
- [ ] You know which product sells best (that becomes your ad creative)
- [ ] You've confirmed Printful quality is consistent

When ready, start with:
1. Retargeting only (people who visited but didn't buy) — cheapest, highest ROI
2. Lookalike audience from your first 50 customers
3. Budget: $10-20 AUD/day maximum to start
