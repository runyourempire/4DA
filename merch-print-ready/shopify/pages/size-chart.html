How to Measure

Body Width: Measured across the chest, 1" below armhole, with garment laid flat.

Body Length: Measured from the high point of the shoulder to the bottom hem.

Sleeve Length: Measured from center back neck to the end of the sleeve (crewneck/hoodie) or from shoulder seam to cuff (tee).

Fit Tips

  • All items are unisex classic fit — not slim, not oversized
  • If you're between sizes, size up
  • Tees are 100% preshrunk cotton — minimal additional shrinkage
  • Crewnecks and hoodies are 50/50 cotton-poly — virtually no shrinkage
  • For an oversized/relaxed look, go 1-2 sizes up
