Shipping Policy

Production Time

All items are custom-printed after you order. Production typically takes 2-5 business days.

Shipping Times

Destination Estimated Delivery
Australia 5-10 business days
New Zealand 7-14 business days
United States / Canada 7-16 business days
Europe / UK 7-20 business days
Rest of World 10-25 business days

These are estimates. Actual delivery times may vary due to carrier delays, customs processing, or high-volume periods.

Shipping Rates

Shipping is calculated at checkout based on your location and order size. Rates are determined by our fulfillment partner, Printful.

Tracking

You will receive a shipping confirmation email with a tracking number once your order is dispatched. Allow 24-48 hours for tracking to update after dispatch.

Customs & Import Duties

International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes. These are the responsibility of the buyer and are not included in the product price or shipping cost.

Lost or Delayed Packages

If your order hasn't arrived within the estimated timeframe, contact us at support@4da.ai with your order number. We'll investigate and resolve the issue.
