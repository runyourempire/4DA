Privacy Policy

4DA Systems Pty Ltd (ACN 696 078 841) operates this store. This policy explains how we handle your information.

What We Collect

When you place an order, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Shipping address
  • Payment information (processed securely by Shopify Payments / Stripe — we never see or store your card details)

How We Use It

  • To fulfill and ship your order (via our print partner, Printful)
  • To send order confirmation and shipping updates
  • To respond to customer support requests

What We Share

We share your shipping details with Printful (our print-on-demand fulfillment partner) solely to produce and deliver your order. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Payment Security

All payments are processed through Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe). Your payment information is encrypted and handled in compliance with PCI-DSS standards. We never have access to your full card number.

Cookies

This store uses essential cookies required for the shopping cart and checkout to function. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising pixels.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us at support@4da.ai.

Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Changes will be posted on this page.
