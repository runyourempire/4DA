Care Instructions

Your 4DA merch is built to last. Follow these guidelines to keep your prints sharp and your fabric fresh.

Washing

  • Turn inside out before washing — this protects the print
  • Machine wash cold (30C / 86F or below)
  • Use a gentle cycle
  • Wash with similar colors
  • Do not use bleach or fabric softener on printed areas

Drying

  • Tumble dry on low heat or hang dry
  • Do not dry clean
  • Remove promptly from the dryer to reduce wrinkles

Ironing

  • Do not iron directly on the print
  • If needed, iron inside out on a low setting
  • Steam is safe for the fabric but avoid steaming directly over prints
