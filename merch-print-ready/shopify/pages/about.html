About 4DA SYSTEMS

4DA stands for 4-Dimensional Autonomy — the pursuit of control over your time, your tools, your data, and your income.

We're a developer tools company based in Brisbane, Australia. We build privacy-first intelligence software that helps developers cut through the noise and focus on what actually matters.

Our merch isn't an afterthought. It's the physical layer of a philosophy: all signal, no feed.

What We Believe

  • Privacy first — your data stays on your machine
  • Signal over noise — less content, more relevance
  • Zero gatekeeping — STREETS (our developer income playbook) is free, forever
  • Build in public — our core app is open source

The Merch

Every piece is designed in-house. We don't do drops, we don't do hype. We make things we'd actually wear — minimal, considered, and built to last.

All items are printed on demand using premium Gildan blanks with DTFlex and DTG printing for vivid, durable results. We ship worldwide from fulfillment centers closest to you.

STREETS

STREETS is the Developer Income Playbook — 7 modules that teach developers to build income beyond the salary. It's free inside the 4DA app. The STREETS tee is for those who are on the path.
