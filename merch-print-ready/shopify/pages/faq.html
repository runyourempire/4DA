Frequently Asked Questions

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

All items are printed on demand after you order. Production takes 2-5 business days. After that, shipping times are:

  • Australia: 5-10 business days
  • New Zealand: 7-14 business days
  • United States / Canada: 7-16 business days
  • Europe / UK: 7-20 business days
  • Rest of World: 10-25 business days

Do you offer free shipping?

Yes. Orders over $120 AUD qualify for free standard shipping worldwide. That's just two tees or one hoodie plus a tee.

Will I get a tracking number?

Yes. You'll receive an email with tracking information once your order ships. Allow 24-48 hours for tracking to update after dispatch.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes. We ship worldwide. International orders may be subject to customs duties and import taxes, which are the responsibility of the buyer.

Can I cancel my order?

Orders can be cancelled within 2 hours of placement, before production begins. After production starts, cancellations aren't possible because each item is printed specifically for you.

Products

What blanks do you use?

Tees: Gildan 5000 — 5.3 oz heavyweight, 100% preshrunk cotton. Classic unisex fit.
Crewneck: Gildan 18000 — 8.0 oz, 50/50 cotton-poly fleece.
Hoodie: Gildan 18500 — 8.0 oz, 50/50 cotton-poly fleece with double-lined hood.

What print method do you use?

Depends on the design. Our sun/photographic designs use DTFlex printing for vivid, durable full-color reproduction. Our typography and logo pieces use DTG (Direct-to-Garment) printing. Both methods are durable and wash-safe.

Will the print crack or peel?

No. Both DTFlex and DTG prints are embedded into the fabric. We recommend washing inside-out in cold water and tumble drying on low to maximize longevity.

What sizes do you carry?

All products are available from S to 5XL. Check our Size Chart for detailed measurements.

Are mockup images exact?

Product images are digital mockups. The final printed product may vary slightly due to screen displays, printing processes, and garment batches. Colors and placement are as accurate as possible.

Returns & Issues

Can I return my order?

Because all products are custom-printed to order, we can't accept returns for change of mind. However, we will replace or refund your order if it arrives damaged, misprinted, or is the wrong item.

What if my order arrives damaged?

Email support@4da.ai within 14 days with your order number and a photo of the issue. We'll send a replacement or issue a full refund within 48 hours.

What if my package is lost?

Contact us with your order number. We'll investigate with the carrier and either reship or refund.
